Leave Policy

Ministry of Information, Communications and The Digital Economy

Leave Entitlements
  • Annual Leave: 30 working days per year
  • Sick Leave: 30 working days with full pay
  • Maternity Leave: 90 calendar days with full pay
  • Paternity Leave: 14 calendar days with full pay
  • Compassionate Leave: 7 working days per occurrence
Application Procedure
  1. Submit leave application at least 30 days in advance (except for emergencies)
  2. Applications must be approved by your immediate supervisor
For full policy details, please refer to the Public Service Commission guidelines.
