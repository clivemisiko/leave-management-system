About MICDE

Ministry Overview

The Ministry of Information, Communication and The Digital Economy (MICDE) is responsible for ICT policy, broadcasting, telecommunications, and digital economy matters in Kenya.

Our Mission

To transform Kenya into a knowledge-based economy through innovative ICT policies and digital infrastructure development.

Leave Management System

This system was developed to streamline staff leave management across all departments of the ministry, ensuring:

  • Efficient leave application processing
  • Transparent leave records
  • Real-time leave status tracking
System Users

The system serves all ministry staff including:

  • Permanent staff
  • Contract employees
  • Department heads
  • HR administrators
System Features
  • Online leave applications
  • Leave balance tracking
  • Mobile accessibility
